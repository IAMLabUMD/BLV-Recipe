# Pasta Carbonara Recipe for Blind and Visually Impaired Cooks
Pasta Carbonara is a classic Italian dish made with pasta, bacon, eggs, and Parmesan cheese. This recipe serves six people and can be prepared in about 25 minutes, with an estimated total time commitment of about 50 minutes for blind and visually impaired cooks.

## Introduction and Overview
This recipe is broken down into 8 steps, each with detailed instructions to help you prepare a delicious Pasta Carbonara. Before starting, make sure you have all the necessary ingredients and tools.

## Ingredients
- 12 ounces of pasta, any variety (about 3 cups of cooked pasta)
- 8 pieces of thick cut bacon, diced small (about 1 cup of diced bacon)
- 1 medium onion, diced small (about 1 cup of diced onion)
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced (about 2 tablespoons of minced garlic)
- 3 whole eggs
- 3/4 cup of finely grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 cup of heavy cream
- Salt and plenty of black pepper
- 1/2 cup of peas

## Instructions
### Step 1: Cook Pasta
Cook the 12 ounces of pasta according to the package directions. Use a talking timer to keep track of the cooking time.

Recipe Tip: Make sure to reserve a cup or two of the pasta water before draining the pasta, as it will be used later to achieve the right consistency for the sauce.

### Step 2: Prepare Bacon
While the pasta is cooking, fry the 8 pieces of diced bacon in a pan over medium heat until it is just barely crisp. Remove the bacon from the pan and drain it on paper towels. Pour off all the bacon grease from the pan, but do not clean it. Return the pan to the stove over medium-low heat.

TBK Tools: Long Wood Spoon, Straining System

### Step 3: Sauté Onions and Garlic
Add the 1 medium diced onion and 2 cloves of minced garlic to the pan. Cook until they are golden brown, stirring occasionally. You should hear a slight sizzle as they cook.

Recipe Tip: Stir the onions and garlic frequently to prevent burning, and use the sound of the sizzling to gauge their doneness.

### Step 4: Prepare Egg Mixture
In a bowl, mix together the 3 whole eggs, 3/4 cup of finely grated Parmesan cheese, 1/2 cup of heavy cream, salt, and plenty of black pepper until smooth.

TBK Tools: Adaptive Measuring Cups and Spoons, Whisk

### Step 5: Combine Pasta and Egg Mixture
When the pasta is done, drain it and place it in a bowl. While the pasta is still really hot, slowly drizzle in the egg mixture, stirring the pasta the whole time. The sauce will become thick and should coat the pasta. Splash in a little hot pasta water if needed for consistency.

Recipe Tip: Use the sound of the sauce thickening and the feel of the pasta to gauge the right consistency. If the sauce is too thick, add a little more pasta water.

### Step 6: Add Peas, Bacon, and Sautéed Onion/Garlic
Halfway through adding the sauce, add the 1/2 cup of peas, the cooked bacon, and the sautéed onion and garlic. Finish adding the sauce, stirring until it's all combined.

### Step 7: Season and Serve
Season the pasta with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan cheese.

Recipe Tip: Use a talking scale to measure the ingredients, and a tactile marker to label the containers.

### Step 8: Plating and Garnish
To plate the pasta, use a serving spoon to portion out the desired amount onto a plate. Use a fork to arrange the pasta in a neat and tidy manner. Garnish with extra Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of black pepper.

TBK Tools: Serving Spoon, Fork, Grater

Enjoy your delicious Pasta Carbonara!
