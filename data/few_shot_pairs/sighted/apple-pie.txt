  Title      : Apple Pie I
  Servings   : 6–8
  Prep Time  : 45 minutes
  Cook Time  : 60 minutes
  Difficulty : Cookbook  |  Recipes  |

  INGREDIENTS  (12 items)
    [Ingredients]
    [Crust]
      - 8  oz  (225  g ) plain  flour
      - 4 oz (110 g)  margarine
      - 2 oz (55 g)  lard
      - 1  pinch   salt
      - 2  tbsp  cold  water
    [Filling]
      - 1  lb  (500 g)  apples , sliced
      - 2 tbsp  lemon juice
      - 1 oz (28 g) salted  butter
      - 2½  cup   sugar , and additional for sprinkling
      - ¼ cup  flour
      - 2½ tbsp  cornstarch
    [Topping]
      - 1 oz (28 g)  milk

  STEPS  (18)
     1. Rub flour, margarine, lard, and salt together until the mixture has the consistency of fine breadcrumbs.
     2. Add 2 tablespoons water to make a soft but firm dough.
     3. Roll pastry on floured surface to the size you want your pie. Make two crusts.
     4. Place one crust in  pie pan .
     5. Peel and slice apples into ⅛ to ¼- inch  (about 5 mm) thick segments.
     6. Add lemon juice to apples.
     7. Mix apples by hand with ½ cup sugar and a few dashes of cinnamon.
     8. Transfer segments, minus their acquired juices, to pie pan. Omitting the expressed juice will help prevent the pie from boiling over in the oven.
     9. Mix the rest of the sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, flour, and cornstarch in a separate bowl.
    10. Pour this over the apples in pie pan.
    11. Dot with butter.
    12. Cover with the other crust.
    13. Firm down edges with fork or finger.
    14. Cut heart or diamond-shaped vents into top crust.
    15. Brush  the pastry top with milk and sprinkle additional sugar and cinnamon over the top.
    16. Bake  at 350 °F (180 °C) until golden brown (about 60 minutes.)
    17. Take the pie out of the oven and let it cool slightly on the counter. Do not try to eat it right out of the oven, as it will be very hot and this can cause burns!
    18. Serve warm with ice cream or  custard .

  NOTES  (4)
      - Use a variety of apples to get more complex flavors and textures.
      - Use 2–3 Granny Smith apples, in combination with the other apples.
      - Replace the lard with shortening for a vegan dessert.
      - Do not precook apples, as this will deprive the pie of a substantial amount of flavor and turns the apples into mush!
